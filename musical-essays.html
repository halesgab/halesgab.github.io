Below is a collection of Spotify playlists created to accompany Bruno Rose Apparel and the photographs/designs that go along with it. Each playlist was designed to work with some piece or idea. Please read descriptions for more information.

Thank you for viewing.

Gabe Hales

BrunoRose Apparel


'LOVE IS DEAD' SOUNDTRACK

Created with the idea of accompanying the 1SS19 collection, 'LOVE IS DEAD'. Includes a wide range of songs, all showcasing, in my opinion, the feeling of love, experience, life, creation and death.

Enjoy.


'LOVERS DIE YOUNGER' SOUNDTRACK

A playlist created to go side by side with the BrunoRose 'LOVERS DIE YOUNGER' Fall Collection. These were the songs that were played while designing and publishing this drop so I thought it was fitting to include them.

Enjoy.


BRUNO ROSE 001 SOUNDTRACK

An accompaniment to the first official summer selection (2018) drop. Featuring an array of sounds that I feel capture the feeling of this selection and its designs perfectly. 

Enjoy.


2AM

A playlist created with the idea of long thought sessions. Like the title entails, this was inspired by those long late night car rides you sometimes find yourself on, thinking about anything and everything. 

Enjoy.


GABE HALES MAG ISSUE001

Created for my first ever self published literary journal entitled 'Gabe Hales Mag'. But why have music to go along with a magazine? To me, music goes hand in hand with almost everything in life, but I feel it complements and exemplifies photography and poetry/writing the most. I feel the perfect way to read and view my work is with music quietly playing in the background.

Enjoy.
