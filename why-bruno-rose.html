Why BrunoRose?


My great grandfather, Armand Bruno, planted a single rose bush every spring and fall for the majority of his life.

After his passing in late 2013, a singular rose bloomed in our family's garden. We took this as a sign of safety and closure.

I, however, felt this tradition needed continuing. With BrunoRose Apparel, my great grandfathers roses can continue to bloom throughout the seasons.


Who am 'I'?


I am Gabe Hales, a college student who runs this entire project out of my apartment in Michigan.
