Yes! I do, but sadly the cost is a bit more. However, I am working on lowering that cost in the future!


When will my shirt ship?

Most shirts ship within 2-4 business day as all shirts are printed per order. This also means not one single Bruno Rose shirt is the same!


Why haven't I gotten a shipping confirmation yet?

Because BrunoRose is entirely run by one person, myself, I send out every shipping notification for each order. Some days a large amount of orders ship and life tends to get in the way as I have a job and full time school aside from running this company. This means that at times the confirmation email can be delayed by a day or two, but it will come eventually! If you're really hankering for your tracking number, just send me a quick email or contact me via Instagram.


I just received my shirt and its not as soft as I expected it to be?

Do not worry! Since every item is printed specifically for that order, they are immediately put into packaging and shipped out, which means after printing they are not washed or dried as to let the ink settle and get the package to you as soon as possible. In order to get your shirt feeling as soft as a babies bottom (and get rid of any leftover water-based ink splatter), just throw it in the washer and dryer for a normal cycle!
